Eight Brazilian capitals concentrate bar, live music, and restaurant options in specific neighborhoods, according to a survey by rental platform QuintoAndar released in January 2025. The selection includes Rio de Janeiro, São Paulo, Salvador, Florianópolis, Belo Horizonte, Porto Alegre, Curitiba, and Brasília.

## Rio de Janeiro

In the capital of Rio de Janeiro state, Lapa remains the central bohemian hub, home to venues like Circo Voador and Rio Scenarium. Santa Teresa and the South Zone neighborhoods complement the offering with bars and pubs.

- Lapa: Circo Voador, Fundição Progresso, Rio Scenarium
- Santa Teresa: Bar do Mineiro, Armazém São Thiago
- Copacabana and Ipanema: Pavão Azul, Empório 37

## São Paulo

Vila Madalena leads nightlife searches, with bars such as Astor and O Pasquim. Pinheiros and Itaim Bibi broaden the options with rooftops and gastrobars.

- Vila Madalena: Bar Astor, Quintal do Espeto
- Pinheiros: Bar do Juarez, Tetto Rooftop Lounge
- Itaim Bibi and Vila Olímpia: Baleia Rooftop, Eu Tu Eles Bar

## Salvador

Rio Vermelho is the most cited district, with establishments like Blue Praia Bar and Boteco do Paulista. Itapuã and Pituba also appear with beachside options.

- Rio Vermelho: Blue Praia Bar, Parador Z1 Bar
- Itapuã: Alerta Praia Bar, Bar do Mamão
- Pituba: SP20 Restolounge, Invictus Music Bar

## Florianópolis

Lagoa da Conceição gathers waterside bars, while Jurerê houses beach clubs. Santo Antônio de Lisboa offers quieter alternatives.

- Lagoa da Conceição: Bar do Boni, Rufus Bar Cervejas Experimentais
- Jurerê: Bar do Cris, Open Lounge
- Santo Antônio de Lisboa: Caminho dos Açores, Rose Sunset Bar

## Belo Horizonte

Savassi and Santa Tereza lead the scene, with a focus on botecos and live music bars. The Centro preserves historic addresses, and Buritis has more reserved pubs.

- Savassi: Laicos Bar, Redentor Bar
- Santa Tereza: Bolota's Bar, Bar do Bolão
- Centro: Bar da Esquina, Del Ruim

## Porto Alegre

Cidade Baixa remains the bohemian reference of the capital of Rio Grande do Sul. Moinhos de Vento and Bom Fim diversify the itinerary with exclusive and alternative bars.

- Cidade Baixa: Boteco Pedrini, Divina Comédia
- Moinhos de Vento: Dionisia Vinhobar e Restaurante
- Bom Fim: Alameda Bom Fim

## Curitiba

Batel concentrates pubs and cocktail bars, while Largo da Ordem blends history with nightlife venues. Rebouças and Bigorrilho complete the offering.

- Batel: Sheridan's Irish Pub, TAJ Bar
- Largo da Ordem and São Francisco: Quintal do Monge, Tangie
- Rebouças: Layout80

## Brasília

The South and North Wings gather bars and live music venues with diverse crowds. The South Sports Clubs Sector houses establishments focused on sports and nightlife.

- Asa Sul: Calaf, O'Rilley Irish Pub
- Setor de Clubes Sul: sports-oriented venues and themed bars
