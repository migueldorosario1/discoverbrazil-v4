A survey published in 2024 by the website O Cafezinho presented nightlife options in eight capitals across Brazil. The research identified venues such as music houses, bars, and pubs in cities from different regions, featuring programs aimed at various audiences.

In Rio de Janeiro, the neighborhoods of Lapa and Barra da Tijuca are home to places like Circo Voador and Clube dos Democráticos. In São Paulo, the study highlighted Lions Night Club in the city center and Kia Ora Pub in Itaim Bibi.

Florianópolis noted the main nightlife spots as Lagoa da Conceição and Avenida Beira-Mar Norte, with bars such as The Black Swan Pub and De Raiz. In Maceió, the survey pointed out Maikai Show Bar and A Bodega, both located near the urban coastline.

Belo Horizonte offers options spread between Savassi and Vila da Serra, featuring Bwana Pub and the venue Josefine. Natal includes forró venues Arrasta-Pé and Poty Bar in the areas of Ponta Negra and Parque das Dunas.

Porto Alegre has options in the neighborhoods of Moinhos de Vento and Cidade Baixa, where Feelings Club Bar and Bodega Bar and Danceteria are located. Curitiba was noted as the Southern capital with the highest presence of electronic music clubs, including Vibe, Eon, and Pulse in the Batel and Rebouças neighborhoods.

O Cafezinho also reported that the study includes additional recommendations for cultural and environmental nighttime tours in other regions, such as the Urban Legends tour in Porto Alegre and the Moon Bath in the natural pools of Maceió.
