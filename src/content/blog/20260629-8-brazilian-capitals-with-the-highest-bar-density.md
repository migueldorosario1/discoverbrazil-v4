In 2024, the portal O Cafezinho compiled a nightlife guide featuring eight Brazilian capitals. The survey identifies neighborhoods where bars, concert halls, and gastrobars are concentrated, with recommendations for establishments in each region.

## Rio de Janeiro and São Paulo

In Rio de Janeiro, Lapa houses venues such as Circo Voador and Rio Scenarium. Santa Teresa has bars like Bar do Mineiro and Armazém São Thiago. In Copacabana and Ipanema, Boteco Belmonte and Pavão Azul are traditional options.

- Lapa: Circo Voador, Fundição Progresso, Rio Scenarium, Booze Bar
- Santa Teresa: Bar do Mineiro, Armazém São Thiago, Portella
- Copacabana and Ipanema: Boteco Belmonte, Pavão Azul, Empório 37

In São Paulo, Vila Madalena gathers themed bars such as Bar Astor and Quintal do Espeto. Itaim Bibi and Vila Olímpia offer rooftops like Baleia Rooftop. Pinheiros features Bar do Juarez and Tetto Rooftop Lounge.

- Vila Madalena: Bar Astor, O Pasquim, Pé de Manga
- Itaim Bibi and Vila Olímpia: Baleia Rooftop, Eu Tu Eles Bar
- Pinheiros: Bar do Juarez, Gaarden Bar, Tetto Rooftop Lounge

## Salvador and Florianópolis

Salvador has Rio Vermelho as a starting point, with bars like Blue Praia Bar and Boteco do Paulista. Barra and Ondina offer Barravento and Camarote Bar. In Itapuã, Alerta Praia Bar and Bar do Mamão are seaside options.

- Rio Vermelho: Blue Praia Bar, Boteco do Paulista, Parador Z1 Bar
- Barra and Ondina: Barravento, Camarote Bar
- Itapuã: Alerta Praia Bar, Bar do Mamão

Florianópolis concentrates its nightlife around Lagoa da Conceição, with Bar do Boni and Rufus Bar. Jurerê Internacional has beach clubs such as Open Lounge. The historic center offers Botequim Floripa and Liquorice Gastropub.

- Lagoa da Conceição: Bar do Boni, Bar de Raiz, Rufus Bar
- Jurerê: Bar do Cris, Open Lounge
- Centro: Botequim Floripa, Liquorice Gastropub, 41 Brooklyn

## Belo Horizonte and Other Capitals

Belo Horizonte, known as the "capital of bars," has Savassi as its main hub, with Laicos Bar and Dalva Botequim Musical. The Lourdes neighborhood features AA Wine Experience and Mercearia 130. Santa Tereza maintains a bohemian atmosphere.

- Savassi: Laicos Bar, Dalva Botequim Musical, Redentor Bar
- Lourdes: AA Wine Experience, Mercearia 130
- Santa Tereza: traditional bohemian bars

Curitiba gathers pubs in Batel and cultural life around Largo da Ordem. Porto Alegre has Cidade Baixa with nightclubs and Moinhos de Vento with bistros. Brasília, in Asa Sul, offers gastronomic options.

- Curitiba: Batel (pubs), Largo da Ordem (cultural life)
- Porto Alegre: Cidade Baixa (nightclubs), Moinhos de Vento (bistros)
- Brasília: Asa Sul (gastronomy)
