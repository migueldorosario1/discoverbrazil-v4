Deep in the Brazilian Amazon, along the winding Rio Jauaperi, lies Xixuaú — a community-based ecotourism destination that has earned certification from WWF and the National Association for Responsible Tourism. According to amazontrip.info, this remote village offers travelers an authentic encounter with untouched rainforest and traditional riverine life.

The journey to Xixuaú is an adventure in itself. Visitors depart from Manaus by boat, cruising the dark waters of the Rio Negro before entering the narrower channels of the Jauaperi. Along the way, pink river dolphins surface alongside the vessel, and flocks of macaws and herons cross the sky. The trip reveals the vast maze of Amazon waterways, setting the stage for an immersive experience.

What sets Xixuaú apart is its 100% community-owned model. Local residents manage every aspect of tourism, from guiding expeditions to running the lodge. This means that your visit directly supports the well-being of the inhabitants and strengthens their stewardship of the forest. As amazontrip.info explains, the project delivers wider community benefits while offering visitors deep insight into local culture.

To minimize environmental impact, groups are kept small. All excursions are conducted on foot, by paddle canoe, or using electric motor boats — no noisy outboards disturb the wildlife. Accommodation consists of wooden bungalows built with traditional indigenous techniques and natural materials. The community draws water from a fresh spring, treating it with solar-powered filtration and Biosand technology before distribution. A year-round waste collection and recycling program keeps the area pristine.

Xixuaú has gained international recognition. Both WWF and Lonely Planet recommend it as one of the best tourist destinations in the Amazon. Travelers who make the journey find themselves in a place where the forest remains largely unspoiled and the rhythms of daily life follow the river.

By visiting Xixuaú, you contribute to the knowledge and protection of the Amazon. Your presence validates the value of standing forest over cleared land, and your spending empowers a community that has chosen conservation. For you, it will undoubtedly be an unforgettable encounter with one of the planet's last great wildernesses.
